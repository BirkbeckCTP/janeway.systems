About

A brief timline of Janeway's development. For more information checkout the about page.

  • June 2017

    Development on Janeway Begins

    Professor Martin Eve and Andy Byers begin working on Janeway to develop a platform for use by the Open Library of Humanities.

  • January 2018

    Orbit Migrates to Janeway

    Orbit: A Journal of American Literature migrates onto Janeway and begins publishing articles.

  • Spring 2018

    Janeway as a Service

    The team are approached by the University of Huddersfield and Iowa State University about a hosted offering and launch their sites soon after. Mauro Sanchez joins the team.

  • 2019-2022

    Expansion

    The Janeway hosted service offering increases to over 30 clients around the world. Joseph Muller joins the team from Michigan Publishing.

Services

We offer various services including hosting and installation of Janeway.

Janeway as a Service

We host and maintain an install of Janeway for you in the cloud so you don't have to.

Installation

Want to host Janeway yourself? Great! Janeway is available under the AGPL License. We can help you install if locally.

Customisations

Sponsor our team to develop new features that you want or need.
